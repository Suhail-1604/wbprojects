Sun flower

Sunflowers, belonging to the genus Helianthus, are renowned for their large, yellow blooms and rapid growth, reaching heights of 5 to 12 feet. Cultivated globally, they symbolize positivity and happiness and have economic significance, with seeds used for snacks and oil. Sunflowers contribute ecologically by supporting wildlife and attracting pollinators. Easy to grow, they are cherished in gardens for their beauty and adaptability. In addition to their ornamental value, sunflowers play a versatile and beloved role in various aspects of culture and ecology.
